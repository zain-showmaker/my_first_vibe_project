          <div class="wiki-body">
            <p><strong>Elden Ring</strong> is a 2022 action role-playing game developed by <em>FromSoftware</em> and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Directed by Hidetaka Miyazaki with world-building by novelist George R. R. Martin, it is considered a landmark in the genre and one of the greatest games ever made.</p>
            <p>The game takes place in the <strong>Lands Between</strong>, a vast open world ruled by demigods who possess fragments of the shattered <em>Elden Ring</em>. Players assume the role of a <strong>Tarnished</strong> — an exile who has been called back to the Lands Between by the guidance of grace — on a quest to restore the Elden Ring and become the Elden Lord.</p>
            <div class="quote-block">"I want players to feel a sense of awe and wonder as they explore this new world. Something that feels both mysterious and familiar at the same time." — <strong>Hidetaka Miyazaki</strong></div>
            <p>Elden Ring received universal acclaim, with critics praising its open-world design, rich lore, challenging but rewarding combat, and stunning environments. It sold over <strong>25 million copies</strong> worldwide and won numerous Game of the Year awards, including at The Game Awards 2022.</p>
          </div>
        </div>

        <div class="wiki-section">
          <div class="ws-title">Gameplay</div>
          <div class="wiki-body">
            <p>Elden Ring follows the action RPG formula pioneered in FromSoftware's <em>Soulsborne</em> series, with players exploring interconnected environments, fighting challenging enemies, and managing stamina in combat. The game introduces a fully open world for the first time in the series.</p>
            <p>Players can freely explore six major regions, each ruled by a demigod boss. The game features over <strong>100 bosses</strong>, dozens of weapon types with unique move sets, six base character classes, and extensive character customization through stats, equipment, and <em>Ashes of War</em> — special abilities that can be assigned to weapons.</p>
            <p>New mechanics include <strong>Spirit Ashes</strong> (summonable AI companions), <strong>mounted combat</strong> on Torrent the spectral steed, and a vastly expanded stealth system compared to previous FromSoftware titles.</p>

        <div class="wiki-section">
          <div class="ws-title">Development History</div>
          <div class="wiki-body">
            <p>Elden Ring was first announced at <em>E3 2019</em>. FromSoftware president Hidetaka Miyazaki revealed the collaboration with George R. R. Martin, who crafted the foundational mythology and history of the Lands Between, a period before the events of the game called the <strong>"Age of the Erdtree."</strong></p>
            <p>The game entered full development in 2019 after Dark Souls: Remastered and Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice shipped. It was released on <strong>February 25, 2022</strong>, following a one-month delay from its original January release window due to additional polish.</p>
